StudyOS

A brutalist, single-file study interface. Track learning loops and revisions completely offline without SaaS lock-in.

Single-File Portability

The entire application logic compiles into a single HTML file you can run from anywhere, forever.

Local Data Only

Your notes, flashcards, and spaced repetition history are stored purely in your browser's IndexedDB.

AI Tutor Integration

Connects directly to the local Zayvora instance to act as a Socratic tutor—all without hitting the network.

Zero Distractions

A minimalist UI designed to keep you in flow state, free from notifications and cloud sync errors.
