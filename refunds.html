Refund Policy

1. General Policy

Due to the cryptographic and offline nature of our sovereign software licenses, refunds are generally evaluated on a case-by-case basis. If a generated passport or environment token fails to authenticate due to our system errors within 14 days of purchase, you are eligible for a full refund.

2. Processing

Approved refunds are processed through our payment gateway (Razorpay) and typically reflect in your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

3. Contact Us

To request a refund, please email dharam@viadecide.com with your transaction ID and the email associated with your Aporaksha Identity.
